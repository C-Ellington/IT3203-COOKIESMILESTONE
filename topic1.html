Different Types of Cookies

Not all cookies are the same. Some are helpful for website functions, while others are used for collecting information.

Cookie Comparison Table

Type Purpose Example
Session Cookie Works while the browser is open Keeps items in a shopping cart
Persistent Cookie Stays on the device for a set time Remembers login settings
Third-Party Cookie Created by another service Ad tracking across websites
Essential Cookie Supports important site functions Allows secure sign-in

Key Points

  1. Session cookies usually disappear when the browser closes.
  2. Persistent cookies can stay for days, weeks, or months.
  3. Third-party cookies are often the most controversial.
  4. Essential cookies are commonly needed for basic website features.
