Why Privacy Can Be a Problem

Cookies can collect browsing habits, interests, and usage patterns. If websites or advertisers combine enough data, they can build a detailed profile of a user.

Examples of Risks

  • Tracking users across many websites
  • Sharing data with advertising companies
  • Storing personal preferences without users understanding it
  • Using data for targeted ads
  • Making users accept data collection without clearly understanding the choices
